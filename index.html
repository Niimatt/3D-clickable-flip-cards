Red Velvet Cake

Moist & Classic

Ingredients

  • Flour, Sugar, Cocoa
  • Buttermilk, Eggs, Oil
  • Red food coloring, Vinegar,Vanila

Instructions

1. Mix dry and wet ingredients.
2. Bake at 350°F for 30 mins.
3. Cool and frost with any frosting of choice.

Chocolate Cake

Rich & Fudgy

Ingredients

  • Cocoa, Flour, Sugar
  • Eggs, Milk, Butter
  • Vanilla extract, Baking soda

Instructions

1. Combine ingredients.
2. Bake at 350°F for 35 mins.
3. Let cool and serve.

Vanilla Cake

Light & Fluffy

Ingredients

  • Flour, Sugar, Baking powder
  • Eggs, Vanilla, Milk
  • Butter

Instructions

1. Mix wet and dry ingredients separately.
2. Pour Dry ingredients into wet.
3. Bake at 350°F for 25 mins.
4. Cool and enjoy!
